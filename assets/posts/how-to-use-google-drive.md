# How to Use Google Drive

![Random image](https://picsum.photos/200)

Google Drive, today, is known as one of the best tools for managing file or user's data. Even though it's been used by people, not many really knows how to utilize it. So what's Google Drive?
## Google Drive?
Google Drive is a cloud file storage from Google that enable users to store file online. It's been established since 2012. Any file that's stored in Google Drive will be accessible anytime and anywhere as long you have an internet connection as it's stored by Google's server not user's device. For free tier Google willingly gives user 15GB storage capacity. Google Drive also didn't restrict any file types so you can store any kind of file.

## Google Drive's Function?
1. Storing file
2. Data/File Backups
3. File sharing
4. File editing collaboration
5. Cross-device access

## How to use?
For your information, Google Drive is available in both app and website formats for mobile and desktop. To use Google Drive, the first step is to ensure that you have a Google account or Gmail account. Without a Google account, you won’t be able to access Google Drive services. Each Google account is given 15 GB of free Google Drive storage. This storage can be expanded, but users need to subscribe. According to Google’s official website, users can get up to 2 TB of Google Drive storage with a subscription starting at around Rp 135,000 per month. Once you have an account, here’s how to use Google Drive:
### Using Google Drive on mobile
- Download the Google Drive app on your phone via the Google Play Store (for Android phones) or the App Store (for iPhone).
- If you already have it, open the Google Drive app on your phone and log in to your Google account.
- Once logged in, tap the add icon and select the file from your phone’s memory that you want to upload and save to Google Drive.
- Any file successfully uploaded to Google Drive can be accessed at any time.


### Using Google Drive on a Laptop:
- Open the “google.com” website in your laptop browser and make sure you are logged in to your Google account.
- On the “google.com” homepage, click the menu located right next to the profile photo and select “Drive”.
- The Google Drive website page will automatically open.
- Click the add icon to upload and save files from your laptop’s memory to Google Drive.
- Users can open files that have been successfully uploaded to Google Drive at any time as desired.
